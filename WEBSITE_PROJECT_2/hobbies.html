HOBBIES

Some of my hobbies include:

  • Playing the Piano. I play the piano and take piano lessons through the Masaki School of music.
  • Swimming. I like to go swimming, whether it is in a pool or snorkeling at beaches around the island. While snorkeling I have seen lots of interesting underwater sea life.
  • Hiking. During the summer I go on hikes with my family. I like to walk through the trees and enjoy the scenery.
  • Tubing. Over the summer we usually go to Texas where we visit family. While we are there we usually go tubing at a lake.
  • Bowling. During this summer, my family and I went bowling frequently. I was able to learn how to walk and bowl.
  • I personally enjoy reading and the piano, and the other hobbies give me a chance to spend quality time with my family.
